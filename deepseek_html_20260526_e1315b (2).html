🏥 Health & Medical Support

Navigating a new healthcare system can be overwhelming. At Viconnect, we help you register with top Kenyan hospitals, find the right doctors, and understand your medical coverage options so you and your family stay healthy and protected.

What We Offer

  • Hospital Registration: We guide you through the paperwork to register with leading hospitals in Nairobi, including Aga Khan University Hospital, Nairobi Hospital, and Kenyatta National Hospital.
  • Insurance Guidance: We help you choose the right medical insurance plan for your needs, from local providers like AAR, CIC, and Jubilee Insurance.
  • Emergency Support: Our 24/7 support team can assist you in medical emergencies, coordinating with hospitals and ambulances for quick care.
  • Wellness Resources: We connect you with pharmacies, clinics, mental health services, and wellness centers throughout Nairobi.

Why Choose Us?

Our team has deep relationships with local healthcare providers and years of experience helping expats and students navigate the system. We prioritize your health and well-being every step of the way.

💰 Banking & Financial Setup

Setting up your finances in a new country can be a challenge. We make it easy by helping you open bank accounts, register for M-Pesa, and understand local financial services so you can manage your money with confidence.

What We Offer

  • Bank Account Setup: We assist you in opening accounts with leading Kenyan banks such as Equity Bank, KCB, and Standard Chartered, handling all the documentation for you.
  • M-Pesa Registration: We guide you through the M-Pesa registration process, explaining how to deposit, withdraw, and send money using Kenya's most popular mobile payment system.
  • Currency Exchange: We provide guidance on the best exchange rates and methods for transferring money internationally to and from Kenya.
  • Tax & Financial Advice: We connect you with local financial advisors who understand the tax obligations for expats and international students in Kenya.

Why Choose Us?

Our financial setup service has helped over 200 expats get their finances sorted within their first week in Kenya. We know the system inside out and save you hours of paperwork and confusion.

🚗 Car Rental & Transport

Getting around Nairobi doesn't have to be stressful. We help you find reliable car rentals, private drivers, and safe transport options tailored to your needs and budget so you can explore the city with confidence.

What We Offer

  • Car Rentals: We partner with trusted local rental agencies to offer you competitive rates on vehicles ranging from compact cars to SUVs and vans for families.
  • Private Drivers: Need a safe, reliable driver for your daily commute or airport pickups? We connect you with vetted, professional drivers who know Nairobi's roads inside out.
  • Airport Transfers: Our airport transfer service ensures you arrive at your accommodation smoothly, with a driver waiting for you at JKIA.
  • Transport Coaching: We teach you how to use Nairobi's public transport system, including matatus, buses, and ride-hailing apps like Uber and Bolt.

Why Choose Us?

We've helped over 300 clients find their perfect transport solution. Our vetted partners and local knowledge mean you get safe, reliable service every time.

🗣️ Language & Communication

Language barriers can make daily life difficult. Our language and communication services help you learn Swahili, access professional translation, and communicate effectively in any situation.

What We Offer

  • Swahili Courses: We offer beginner to advanced Swahili courses, taught by native speakers with experience teaching international students and expats.
  • Translation Services: Need documents translated? Our professional translators can translate contracts, legal papers, academic transcripts, and more.
  • Communication Coaching: We provide coaching on cultural communication styles, business etiquette, and everyday interactions in Kenya.
  • Language Exchange: Join our language exchange program to practice your Swahili with locals while helping them learn English or French.

Why Choose Us?

We understand that language is the key to integration. Our courses and services are designed specifically for expats and students, making learning practical and enjoyable.
